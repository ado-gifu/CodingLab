In the window on the right,
multiple tables of 8-bit color palettes
are displayed and compared side by side.

On the top:
Left: r-g-b consist of 2 bits each.
These can potentially be tightly packed, saving bits.

Middle: RGB pattern is 2-3-3.
This table has very nice gradients of color and rich values.
Due to disligned nature of its fields' values, gray is conditional.
Right: RGB pattern is 3-3-2
